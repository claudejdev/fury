NATO/France/Navy/Aircraft Carriers/CVG Clemenceau

FS Clemenceau (R 98)

The Clemenceau was tied up at Toulon at war start undergoing routine maintenance and training. After three days of rapid preparation she was tasked to the Atlantic to work with the Royal Navy and re-take Vagar. She gathered her BG either in port of enroute through the western Mediterranean.
