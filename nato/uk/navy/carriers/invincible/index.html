HMS Invincible CVG

HMS Invincible (Vince) had completed a minor refit in late 1993 and was conducting fleet training with several RN ships and submarines in the Celtic Sea between Wales and Ireland when the war started. She immediately returned to Portsmouth to replenish and put to sea again within 24 hours, still awaiting orders. Her first task of the war would be an intimidating one, to stop the Soviet advance south of the Shetland and Faro islands into the Atlantic. During the first three days of the war however she had gained a formidable Battle Group, including a slightly over crowded air group.
