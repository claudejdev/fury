CVBG Theodore Roosevelt (CVN-71)

The USS Theodore Roosevelt (CVN-71) is finishing up a four month Scheduled ‘Selected Restricted Availability’ (SRA) maintenance period at Norfolk. She was planning to put to sea again in late February of 94 but hostilities hastened that departure slightly. She and her Battle Group will move north with the amphibious units during the last week of February.
