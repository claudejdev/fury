USS Forrestal (AVT-59)

The USS Forrestal moved to Naval Air Station Pensacola to replace the USS Lexington as the Navy’s training carrier in September of 92 when she was reclassified as an auxiliary and given the title ‘AVT’ (Training Aircraft Carrier). Historically this transition was cancelled early in 93 however, and the USN lost its dedicated training carrier. In Northern Fury the transition continued and by the end of 93 she was fully operational as AVT-59.

Flight operations are conducted by VT-86 Saberhawks equipped with the 24 of the new T-45C Goshawk.
